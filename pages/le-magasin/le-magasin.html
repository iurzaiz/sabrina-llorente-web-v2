Le Magasin
–2016

Illustrations & Packaging design for a Home Decor store in Montreal

MORE INFO –
Le Magasin is a home decor and furniture store with an Artsy Fashion Style. It’s also the perfect spot to find a great gift, for spur of the moment shopping or falling for that unique piece of furniture. Le Magazin carries a large variety of household items such as pillows, frames, vases, lighting, furniture, kitchen items, etc. You can also find lots of cute lifestyle items such as jewelry, candles , bags and scarves. Constantly evolving and looking for the latest trends, Le Magasin offers a unique, warm and inspiring environment.
